Lemon garlic salmon tray bake

Home

This is a tasty salmon tray bake recipe that’s as simple as it is healthy. Salmon is slathered with an assertive lemon garlic paste that adds a stack of flavour, then cooks in just 11 minutes alongside parmesan asparagus and blistered cherry tomatoes. Sheet pan oven dinner. No stove splatter – yay!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Dijon mustard
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Asparagus

Instructions

  1. Mix the lemon garlic paste ingredients together then slather onto the surface and side of the salmon.
  2. Marinade for 1 hour
  3. Toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Prepare tray
  5. Oven grill / broil for 11 minutes or until the salmon is cooked.
